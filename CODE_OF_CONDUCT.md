# Code of Conduct

We follow the **Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct** to ensure a welcoming and inclusive environment.

---

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, ability, ethnicity, or background.

## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior:
- Using inclusive and respectful language
- Being supportive of different viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on the projectâ€™s goals

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or intimidation
- Discriminatory remarks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement
- Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and enforcing them.
- Maintainers may remove or ban contributors violating this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by creating a **confidential issue** or contacting the maintainers.
